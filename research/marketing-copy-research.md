## 7. FAQ Section

### Q1: Is it really free? What's the catch?

**A:** No catch. The website is free forever — not a trial, not a limited version. You get a full professional roofing website with SEO, contact forms, lead capture, and email notifications at no cost. We make money when you choose to add the satellite estimate widget for $99/month. If you never upgrade, you never pay. Simple as that.

### Q2: I'm not a tech guy. Can I actually set this up?

**A:** If you can type your business name and phone number, you can set this up. We're talking 5 minutes. No design decisions, no drag-and-drop builders, no code. Enter your info, pick a color, and your site is live. One roofer told us his daughter did it during lunch.

### Q3: How is this different from Roofle?

**A:** Roofle charges $350/month plus a $2,000 setup fee — that's $6,200 in the first year for just the estimate widget. No website included. RoofReady gives you a free professional website plus the same satellite-powered estimates for $99/month with zero setup fee. Same technology. 71% cheaper. Plus you get a website.

### Q4: Can I use my own domain name?

**A:** Yes. You can connect your existing domain or use the free subdomain we provide (yourbusiness.roofready.com). Custom domain support is included — no extra charge.

### Q5: What if I already have a website?

**A:** You don't need our website to use our estimate widget. The widget embeds on any site with one line of code — WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, whatever you've got. Paste it in and it works. If you want to switch to our free website too, you can do that anytime.

### Q6: How accurate are the satellite estimates?

**A:** We use Google's Solar API — the same satellite data that solar companies use to measure millions of roofs. It measures actual roof area, pitch, and complexity from overhead imagery. Combined with your custom pricing, estimates land within 8-12% of actual contract price. As you calibrate your rates, that tightens to 5-8%. These are ballpark estimates designed to start the conversation, not replace your in-person quote.

### Q7: Is there a contract? What if I want to cancel?

**A:** No contract. Month-to-month. Cancel anytime with one click from your dashboard. No cancellation fees. No phone calls required. No "retention specialist" trying to talk you out of it. Your data and leads are still yours after you cancel.

### Q8: Do I own my website and leads if I leave?

**A:** Yes. Your leads are exportable anytime. Your website content is yours. We don't hold your business hostage. If you leave, you take your stuff with you. That's how it should work.
