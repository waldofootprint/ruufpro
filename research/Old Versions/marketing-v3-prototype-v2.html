Questions? Answers.

Is it really free? What's the catch?
No catch. Your website costs nothing — today, tomorrow, next year. We make money on the optional estimate widget ($99/mo). If you never add it, your free site is still yours.
I'm not a tech person. Can I actually do this?
If you can type your name and phone number, you're good. We generate everything else. Most roofers are live in under 5 minutes.
Who's behind RuufPro?
A small team that saw great roofers losing jobs because they didn't have a website — and the only options were overpriced agencies or $350/mo widgets. We built what should exist for small roofing businesses.
Won't online estimates let homeowners haggle?
You set the prices — not us. A homeowner who sees your price and still reaches out is a better lead than someone blindly shopping. The estimate qualifies them before you pick up the phone.
What if another roofer in my area uses RuufPro?
Each site is unique — your name, your branding, your services. Homeowners choose based on trust, reviews, and how professional you look. Not which platform built your site.
How accurate are the satellite estimates?
They're ballpark estimates, not final quotes. Satellite imagery measures roof area accurately for most homes. Complex rooflines or tree cover can affect precision. That's why we call them "ballpark."
Do I own my leads?
Yes. Every lead is yours. We never sell, share, or give your leads to other roofers. Export anytime.
