Questions? Answers.

The stuff roofers actually want to know.

Is it really free? What's the catch?
Really free. No credit card, no trial period, no "free for 14 days" games. Your website is free forever. We make money when roofers choose to add the instant estimate widget ($99/mo) — but you never have to. The free site is fully functional on its own.
How long does setup actually take?
About 2 minutes. We need your business name, phone number, and city. That's it. We auto-generate everything else — the design, the copy, the SEO, the contact form. You can customize it later from your dashboard, but most roofers are live in under 2 minutes.
Can I use my own domain name?
Coming soon. Right now your site lives at yourname.ruufpro.com, which works great for getting started. Custom domain support is on our roadmap.
What if I already have a website?
You can still use the estimate widget on your existing site — it embeds anywhere. Or try our free site alongside yours and see which gets more leads. No risk either way.
How does the instant estimate widget work?
Homeowners enter their address, and we use satellite imagery to measure their roof. They pick their preferred material, and our calculator generates a ballpark estimate based on YOUR pricing. You set the prices — we just do the math and deliver the lead.
Will this work in my area?
Yes. RuufPro works anywhere in the US. The satellite roof data covers all 50 states. Your website will be optimized for your specific city and service area.
