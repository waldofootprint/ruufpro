## SECTION 11 — FAQ

**Is it really free?**
Yes. Not a trial. Not "free for 14 days." Your website is free — today, tomorrow, next year. We make money on the optional estimate widget ($99/mo). If you never add it, your free site is still yours.

**I'm not a tech person. Can I do this?**
If you can type your name and phone number, you're good. We generate everything else. Most roofers are live in under 5 minutes.

**Who's behind RuufPro?**
A small team that saw great roofers losing jobs because they didn't have a website — and the only options were overpriced agencies or $350/mo widgets. We built what should exist for small roofing businesses.

**Won't online estimates let homeowners haggle me down?**
You set the prices — not us. And a homeowner who sees your price and still reaches out is a better lead than someone blindly shopping around. The estimate qualifies them before you pick up the phone.

**What if another roofer in my area uses RuufPro too?**
Each site is unique — your name, your branding, your services. Homeowners choose based on how professional you look, your reviews, and whether they trust you. Not which platform built your site.

**What about the Google Online Estimates filter?**
Google now lets homeowners filter search results for roofers with online pricing. The free website improves your overall Google visibility. The estimate widget ($99/mo) is what qualifies you for the specific Online Estimates filter.

**What happens to my site if RuufPro shuts down?**
Your leads are always exportable. We'd give notice and transition time. But we're built to last — the free model means we don't need to squeeze customers to survive.

**How accurate are the satellite estimates?**
They're ballpark estimates, not final quotes. Satellite imagery measures roof area accurately for most homes. Complex rooflines or heavy tree cover can reduce precision. That's why we call them "ballpark" — homeowners understand this.

**Can I use my own domain?**
Coming soon. For now, yourname.ruufpro.com. Custom domain support is on our roadmap.

**Do I own my leads?**
Yes. Every lead is yours. We never sell, share, or give your leads to other roofers. Export anytime.
