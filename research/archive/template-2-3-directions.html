My Take

Dark template: I'd go with Dark C (Slate Mono). It's the most versatile of the three — it works for tech-savvy roofers, solar+roofing combos, and anyone who just wants a "modern" dark site. Forge is beautiful but niche (only premium roofers). Ironclad is bold but polarizing (great for commercial, alienating for residential).

Creative template: Creative C (Illustrated) is the sweet spot. It has the hand-drawn pencil energy you mentioned — sketchy underlines, corner marks, handwritten annotations — but the underlying layout is clean and professional. It's creative without being chaotic. Sketchbook is warmer but less structured. Kinetic is impressive but the animations could feel gimmicky for a contractor audience.

The trio: Modern Clean (professional light) + Slate Mono (modern dark) + Illustrated (creative personality) covers every roofer persona without any overlap.
