Part 2: Market Gap Analysis — What We MUST Offer

The 3 Gaps That Define Our Opportunity

Gap 1: No one offers a free roofing website + estimate widget together.
Roofle = widget only (no website). Roofr = CRM + measurements (no website; beta site builder at $199/mo add-on). Scorpion = website + everything ($3-10K/mo, locked in). GoDaddy = generic website (no estimate widget, no lead tools). We fill this: free roofing-specific website + $99/mo estimate widget.
Gap 2: Google's Online Estimates filter has no affordable solution for small roofers.
Roofle ($350/mo + $2K) and Roofr ($249/mo + $149 estimator = $398/mo) serve it but exclude 1-10 person crews. 76% of roofers don't show pricing. 78% of homeowners want it. We fill this: estimate widget at $99/mo — 71% cheaper than Roofle.
Gap 3: Small roofers choose between "too basic" and "too much."
GoDaddy/Wix = too basic (no lead tools). JobNimbus/AccuLynx = too much ($349-549/mo, weeks of setup). We fill this: website + estimates + reviews + auto-reply at $99-149/mo, setup in minutes.
