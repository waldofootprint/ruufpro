## SECTION 9 — FAQ

**Q: Is it really free? What's the catch?**
No catch. Your website costs nothing — today and going forward. Not a trial. Not "free for 14 days." We make money when roofers choose to add the estimate widget for $99/mo. If you never add it, your free site is still yours forever.

**Q: I'm not a tech guy. Can I actually do this?**
If you can type your business name and phone number, you can do this. We generate everything else. Most roofers are live in under 5 minutes.

**Q: Who's behind RuufPro?**
We're a small team that saw a gap: great roofers losing jobs because they don't have a decent website, and the only options being overpriced agencies or $350/mo widgets. We built what we wished existed for small roofing businesses.

**Q: Won't online estimates give homeowners ammunition to haggle?**
You control the pricing — these are YOUR numbers. And a homeowner who sees your price and still fills out the form is a warmer lead than someone shopping blindly. The estimate qualifies them before you ever pick up the phone.

**Q: What if another roofer in my city is also on RuufPro?**
There's room for multiple roofers on the platform. Each site is unique to your business — your name, your services, your pricing. Homeowners choose based on trust signals, reviews, and how professional you look, not which platform you used.

**Q: What about the Google Online Estimates filter?**
Google lets homeowners filter search results to show only roofers with online pricing. Our estimate widget ($99/mo) is designed to meet Google's requirements for this filter. The free website alone improves your general Google visibility but doesn't include the pricing tool needed for the filter.

**Q: What happens to my site if RuufPro shuts down?**
Your leads are always exportable. We're committed to giving notice and transition time. But honestly — we built this to last, and the free model means we don't need to squeeze customers to survive.

**Q: How accurate are the satellite estimates?**
They're ballpark estimates — not final quotes. Satellite imagery measures roof area accurately for most homes. Complex rooflines or heavy tree cover can affect precision. That's why we call them "ballpark estimates," not quotes. Homeowners understand this.

**Q: Can I use my own domain?**
Coming soon. Right now your site lives at yourname.ruufpro.com. Custom domain support is on our roadmap.

**Q: Do I own my leads?**
Yes. Every lead belongs to you. We never sell, share, or give your leads to other roofers. You can export them anytime.
