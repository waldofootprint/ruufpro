# Google's "Online Estimates" Filter — What We Know

Research compiled from Roofle blog, Roofr blog, Demand IQ, RoofersCoffeeShop, RoofingSites.com, and industry sources.

---

## What It Is

Google has added an **"Online Estimates" filter** to local search results for home services including roofing. It appears at the **top of search results — above ads, reviews, and "top-rated" listings.**

When a homeowner clicks this filter, the results narrow to ONLY show contractors who offer real online pricing tools. Everyone else disappears.

## When It Launched

- **Rolled out December 2025** (Roofle blog, Dec 29 2025)
- Described as a "quiet" rollout — no major Google announcement
- By early 2026, Google added "Online estimate" buttons directly in search results
- The filter appears on searches like "roofing companies near me," "roofers in [city]"

## How It Works

1. Homeowner searches for a roofer on Google
2. They see an "Online Estimates" filter button at the top of results
3. Clicking it removes ALL contractors who don't offer online pricing
4. Only contractors with real estimate tools remain visible

**Key quote from Demand IQ:** "When that filter is applied, contractors that don't support online estimates are removed from view."

**This is not a traditional SEO penalty.** It's a filter. But the effect is the same — if a homeowner uses it, you're invisible.

## How Many Homeowners Use It

**1 in 5 (20%) of homebuyers use the filter** when searching for roofing services.
— Source: RoofersCoffeeShop

This number will likely grow as homeowners discover it and as Google promotes it more prominently.

## What Qualifies a Contractor

To show up when the filter is active, contractors need BOTH:

1. **Google Business Profile attribute:** Enable "Online estimates" in GBP → Attributes
2. **A real online estimate experience on their website** that includes:
   - Guided questions scoping the job
   - Real pricing logic (not "fill out a form and we'll call you")
   - Immediate estimate ranges
   - Actual engagement (time on page, completion rates)

**Key quote from Demand IQ:** "Not 'fill out this form and we'll call you.' Not vague generalized pricing ranges." — Google is checking for REAL estimate tools, not fake ones.

## Impact on Contractors WITHOUT Online Estimates

- **Invisible to 20% of high-intent homeowners** right now (growing)
- **"Years of SEO work, review gathering, and thousands of marketing dollars can suddenly stop paying off"** — Demand IQ
- Google monitors engagement signals: short visits, immediate bounces, low engagement can REDUCE ranking even outside the filter
- Contractors who adapted early get algorithmic preference

## Impact on Contractors WITH Online Estimates

- **Visibility boost** in local results — Google is "actively rewarding local service businesses that make it easier for homeowners to get pricing fast" (Roofr)
- **Case study:** Amanda from Maven Roofing uses instant estimates to "land an extra 2+ jobs each month without any extra work" (Roofr)
- Better lead quality — the estimate tool "filters out the folks who won't be able to afford it" and "captures lead info automatically"
