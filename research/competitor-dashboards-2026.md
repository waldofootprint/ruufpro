# Competitor Dashboard Research (March 2026)

Research on what roofing SaaS competitors offer contractors inside their dashboards/portals, plus what small roofers actually want.

---

## 1. Roofr (roofr.com)

**What it is:** All-in-one roofing software -- CRM, measurements, proposals, invoicing, payments.

### Dashboard Screens & Sections

- **Job Pipeline Board** -- Kanban-style columns (New Lead, Appt. Scheduled, Proposal Sent, Pre-Production, etc.). Drag-and-drop cards. Each card shows price, location, assignee, source. Fully customizable stages.
- **Performance Dashboard** -- Real-time analytics: lead sources, job values won vs. lost, sales close rates, team performance, job cost and timeline analysis.
- **Job Reports** -- At-a-glance sales performance and team productivity. Spot bottlenecks without spreadsheets.
- **Proposals** -- Create proposals from measurement reports. Good/Better/Best pricing tiers. E-signature with unlimited signings. Track sent/received/opened status.
- **Measurements** -- Aerial reports via address lookup ($13 flat rate per address, all structures included). DIY measurement tool. Material calculations with waste factors. Reports delivered in ~2 hours.
- **Invoicing & Payments** -- Create invoices from signed proposals. Payment status dashboard. Credit card (2.8% + $0.30) and ACH (0.5%, capped $40). Track outstanding balances.
- **Material Orders** -- Auto-populated customer details. Supplier integration (ABC Supply, Beacon, SRS). Order status tracking. Line-item import from proposals.
- **Calendar** -- Event scheduling tied to job records. Team availability. Automated homeowner notifications.
- **Documents** -- PDF upload with signature field insertion. Tracking (sent, opened, signed).
- **Automations** -- Trigger workflows on status changes, proposal updates, task assignments. Elite plan only.

### Mobile App

Mobile-optimized web app (not native). Can check jobs, update status, manage leads from phone. **Dedicated native app listed as "coming soon" (PWA in roadmap).**

### What Reviewers Love

- Speed: quotes and proposals in minutes, not hours
- Professional-looking proposals boost close rates and homeowner trust
- Simple, clean interface -- easy to learn
- $13 flat-rate measurements are a bargain vs. competitors
- All-in-one: measurements flow into proposals flow into invoices
- 4.6/5 rating, 9.19/10 likelihood to recommend

### What Reviewers Hate

- Pricing tiers feel restrictive as teams scale (per-report costs add up)
- SMS and automations locked to expensive plans
- Pitch measurement fails ~once/month
- Mobile photo uploads have limitations
- CRM still maturing vs. established players (JobNimbus, AccuLynx)
- Missing some material options

### Standout UX

- Unified flow: Instant Estimator -> Measurements -> Proposals -> Material Orders -> Invoices
- Everything connected, minimal re-entry of data
- Clean kanban board is the central hub

## 2. Roofle (roofle.com)

**What it is:** Instant online roof quoting widget (RoofQuote PRO) + contractor portal. Focused on lead gen through instant estimates on contractor websites.

### Dashboard Screens & Sections

- **PRO Portal / Leads Dashboard** -- All instant quotes and leads in one place. Sales, marketing, and production teams each get their own view.
- **Quote Management** -- Every quote shows Total SqFt, Predominant Slope/Pitch, suggested Waste, number of stories, perimeter LF for every structure. AI-suggested waste and slope.
- **Advanced Quoting (Rep Quotes)** -- Custom rep links for sales reps to generate quotes independently. Detailed measurements available.
- **Proposal Builder** -- Convert quotes into e-sign or PDF proposal contracts. Trackable e-signatures. Customizable emails. Send unlimited proposals.
- **Measurement Orders** -- Order EagleView, GAF QuickMeasure, and GeoSpan reports directly within the portal. Automate takeoffs.
- **Material Orders** -- Beacon PRO+ integration. Automate item quantities. Real-time cost forecasting.
- **Financing** -- Built-in Contractor Loan Pro merchant account. 40+ loan products. Homeowners pre-qualify in under a minute without credit impact.
- **Production Dashboard** -- Material ordering and production management tools.

### Mobile App

**Yes -- native app (iOS via App Store, Android via Google Play).** Recently launched ("NEW MOBILE APP"). Leads dashboard, quotes, proposals accessible on the go.

### What Reviewers Love

- The instant quote widget is the killer feature: homeowners get estimates in seconds, 24/7
- Dramatically increases website conversion rates
- Easy to use with responsive customer service
- EagleView integration for accurate measurements
- Financing built right into the quoting flow

### What Reviewers Hate

- Limited independent review data available (newer product)
- Dashboard appears more focused on quote management than full CRM
- Not a full CRM -- contractors still need a separate system for job management, scheduling, etc.

### Standout UX

- The consumer-facing instant quote widget is the defining feature (this is essentially what RuufPro's estimate widget competes with)
- Shopping-like experience for homeowners
- The portal is built around the quote-to-close flow, not general project management

## 3. JobNimbus (jobnimbus.com)

**What it is:** Full CRM and project management platform built for roofing contractors. The market leader by user count (6,000+ companies).

### Dashboard Screens & Sections

- **Boards (Kanban)** -- The signature feature. Visual drag-and-drop boards showing job stages. "Digitized whiteboard" feel. Spot bottlenecks instantly. Multiple boards for different workflows.
- **Contacts/Leads** -- Every lead tracked from first inquiry. Pipeline visibility. Lead source tracking. Automated follow-ups via email and SMS.
- **Jobs & Work Orders** -- Job detail pages with all info, photos, documents, notes. Crew assignment. Subcontractor management. Milestone tracking.
- **Estimates & Proposals** -- Smart estimating with measurement integration. Proposal creation and tracking.
- **Calendar & Scheduling** -- Install scheduling for crews, subs. Calendar view. Automated SMS notifications to homeowners when jobs are scheduled.
- **Communication (Engage)** -- Built-in texting and email. Automated messaging templates. Review request automation. Caller ID integration.
- **Payments (JobNimbus Payments)** -- Credit/debit, ACH processing. Invoice creation. Financing options. Down payment and final payment collection. Reported 25% increase in collections.
- **Profit Tracker** -- Real-time job profitability tracking.
- **Material Ordering** -- QXO/Roof Hub integration.
- **Reports & Automations** -- Custom reports. Classic Reports feature. Workflow automations (trigger actions on task completion).
- **Photo Documentation** -- Capture and annotate photos tied to jobs.

### Mobile App

**Yes -- native iOS and Android app. 4.8 stars with 5,000+ reviews.** Full job management, photo capture with annotations, payment collection, calendar, sales board access.

### What Reviewers Love

- Board view is intuitive and beloved -- feels like a familiar whiteboard
- Single dashboard for contacts, jobs, photos, and payments
- Photo documentation great for insurance claims
- QuickBooks integration works well
- Payment acceleration (25% faster collections reported)
- User-friendly layout, customizable workflows

### What Reviewers Hate

- **Mobile app crashes**, especially on older phones
- **No offline access** -- unusable in rural areas with weak signal
- "Insights" reporting feature widely criticized as "awful"
- Email system described as "terrible"
- Hidden add-on fees (SMS, vendor portals, payment processing)
- Onboarding/support complaints: rushed, then unavailable after setup
- No customer-facing portal
- No job costing or vendor portals
- Learning curve for initial setup and workflow customization

### Standout UX

- The board/kanban view is JobNimbus's identity -- roofers who used whiteboards love it
- Drag-and-drop simplicity
- Best suited for medium teams (5-50 people), can overwhelm solo operators

## 4. AccuLynx (acculynx.com)

**What it is:** Enterprise-grade roofing CRM. The most feature-complete option, built for larger operations. Roofing-only focus.

### Dashboard Screens & Sections

- **Main Dashboard** -- Customizable widgets: "Open Leads," "Jobs in Progress," etc. Color-coded job status (green = on track, red = needs attention). Live activity feed with real-time updates across the company.
- **Sales Pipeline** -- 360-degree view. AI-powered lead intelligence ranks leads by purchase likelihood. Lead API for automated import from web forms.
- **Job Files** -- Centralized document storage per job. Photos with annotations. Permits, supplements, mortgage check tracking. Project milestones.
- **Estimation** -- Integration with EagleView, Hover, Geospan, GAF QuickMeasure. Templates with materials, measurements, and profit margins.
- **Production Management** -- Labor crew scheduling. Delivery calendar. Production and labor cost monitoring.
- **Material Ordering** -- ABC Supply, SRS Distribution, QXO catalogs with real-time pricing. Direct ordering from within the platform.
- **Financial Dashboard** -- Job costing with revenue/expenditure tracking. QuickBooks sync. Sales commission module with approval workflow. Pre-built reporting suite.
- **Customer Portal** -- (Add-on) Document sharing, payment requests, messaging with homeowners.
- **SmartDocs** -- Document management (add-on).
- **Multi-Location Management** -- For companies operating across markets.
- **Calendar & Scheduling** -- Shared calendars, labor scheduling, real-time crew updates.
- **Reporting** -- Pre-built and customizable dashboards. Sales performance, financials, team metrics.

### Mobile App

**Yes -- iOS and Android.** Can access leads, job records, documents, team communication. However, **widely criticized** -- described as "very limited" and "cumbersome to edit estimates." Lacks basic functionality like sales tax handling and field estimate editing.

### What Reviewers Love

- Everything is interconnected: "photos, emails, text, orders, past clients, mapping, numbers, documents, permits"
- Clear pipeline visibility from sale through installation to payment
- Seamless workflow from scheduling to estimates to ordering
- Intuitive once learned, minimal instruction needed
- AI lead ranking is genuinely useful
- Entering job costs at any point in the process

### What Reviewers Hate

- **Expensive** -- per-seat pricing ($60-$120/user/month) punishes growth
- **Nickel-and-dime add-ons**: SmartDocs, texting, customer portals, aerial measurements, material ordering all cost extra
- **Mobile app is bad** -- serious functionality gaps
- Poor reporting capabilities despite having a reporting module
- Limited third-party integrations
- Buggy updates, discontinued features without notice
- Calendar/scheduling redesign made it harder to read at a glance
- Quote-based pricing with no transparency

### Standout UX

- Color-coded job status (green/red) for instant visual priority
- AI lead scoring is unique among roofing CRMs
- Activity feed for company-wide real-time awareness
- Most comprehensive feature set -- but at a cost

## 5. CompanyCam (companycam.com)

**What it is:** Photo documentation and project management app. Not a CRM -- it's the visual layer that integrates with CRMs.

### Dashboard Screens & Sections

- **Company Dashboard** -- Bird's-eye view of productivity: total projects, photo counts, checklist completions, reports created. Filter by time frame. Drill into individual user performance.
- **Projects View** -- Each project gets its own photo timeline. GPS and time-stamped photos organized automatically. Tags, labels for filtering.
- **Photo Tools** -- Unlimited photos/videos. Annotations (drawings, arrows, comments, voice notes). Before/after transformation photos. Photo galleries.
- **Checklists** -- Custom to-do lists per job site. Mark off tasks. Templates for recurring processes.
- **Reports** -- Tailored professional reports. Templates for customers, subs, or insurance companies. AI-powered report generation.
- **User Management** -- Add/manage users. Track individual productivity (photo counts per user). Pending invitations.
- **AI Features** -- AI site walkthroughs, checklists, summaries, daily logs, translations, voice-to-report.

### Mobile App

**Yes -- mobile-first design. Full offline sync.** iOS: 4.8 stars (23K+ reviews). Android: 4.3 stars. This is primarily a mobile app -- the desktop dashboard is secondary.

### What Reviewers Love

- Dead simple to use -- even tech-challenged crew members pick it up fast
- Unlimited photo/video storage on all plans
- GPS/time-stamped photos = accountability and proof of work
- Real-time photo sharing keeps everyone in the loop
- Great for insurance claims and customer communication
- Extensive integration ecosystem (works with JobNimbus, AccuLynx, etc.)
- AI features are genuinely useful for reports

### What Reviewers Hate

- **Pricing**: $79/month minimum (3-user minimum on all plans), expensive per field tech
- Feature bloat over time -- "what once took minutes now takes 10x longer"
- Updates sometimes cause outages/data unavailability
- GPS/address issues: photos sometimes tagged to wrong property
- Customer support unresponsive to direct issues
- Not a CRM -- you still need a separate system

### Standout UX

- Mobile-first design is the gold standard for field apps
- Offline sync means it works on rooftops with no signal
- Photo timeline per project is incredibly intuitive
- Before/after transformation photos are a marketing asset

## What Small Roofers (1-10 Person Crews) Actually Want

Based on industry research, review analysis, and "best roofing software for small contractors" searches:

### Top Priority: Simplicity Over Features

- Small roofers don't want to spend weeks learning software
- "A complex CRM can slow them down more than it helps"
- They want to get off paper and look professional without a steep learning curve
- Jobber-style simplicity (quotes, scheduling, invoicing, customer comms) wins over enterprise features

### The Features That Matter Most

1. **Lead tracking** -- Never lose a lead. Know where every prospect stands. Automated follow-ups.
2. **Fast estimates/proposals** -- Generate professional estimates in minutes, not hours. Aerial measurements are a huge time-saver.
3. **Invoicing and payments** -- Get paid faster. Accept cards and ACH. No chasing checks.
4. **Job status board** -- Visual kanban showing every job's stage. Drag-and-drop.
5. **Scheduling** -- Calendar for crews and installs. Homeowner notifications.
6. **Mobile access** -- Must work on a phone, in the field, on a roof. Offline capability is a major plus.
7. **Photo documentation** -- Before/after, insurance claims, customer proof.
8. **Revenue dashboard** -- See close rates, revenue, and profitability at a glance.

### The Features They Don't Care About (Yet)

- Multi-location management
- AI lead scoring
- Complex commission workflows
- Vendor portals
- Advanced automations (nice-to-have, not need-to-have)

### The #1 Complaint Across All Platforms

**Too many tools, too much complexity.** Contractors are "rethinking their tech stacks by trading niche tools for systems that connect sales, scheduling, and payments in one place." Many started with Roofr for proposals + Hover for measurements + JobNimbus for CRM + separate apps for scheduling and payments = "a cluttered tech stack that takes more time to manage than it saves."

### Pricing Sensitivity

Small roofers are extremely price-sensitive. Per-seat pricing that "punishes growth" is a major pain point. AccuLynx's $60-$120/user/month is a dealbreaker for most small shops. Flat-rate or simple tiered pricing wins.

## Implications for RuufPro Dashboard

Based on this research, the RuufPro dashboard opportunity is:

1. **Start simple.** The biggest gap in the market is a dashboard that's genuinely easy for a 1-3 person crew. JobNimbus and AccuLynx are built for bigger teams. Roofr is getting there but adds complexity as it grows.

2. **The widget is the wedge.** Roofle proves that instant online estimates are a powerful lead-gen hook. RuufPro already has this. The dashboard should be built around managing the leads that the widget generates.

3. **Lead management is job #1.** Every lead from the widget should land in a simple pipeline view. Name, address, estimate details, status, next action. That's the core screen.

4. **Don't try to be a full CRM on day one.** Small roofers don't need commission tracking, multi-location management, or AI lead scoring. They need to see their leads, send proposals, and get paid.

5. **Mobile matters but web-first is fine.** Roofr doesn't have a native app and it's growing fast. A responsive web dashboard that works on phones is sufficient to start.

6. **Photo documentation and scheduling can wait.** CompanyCam owns photos. JobNimbus owns scheduling. These are integration opportunities, not features to build from scratch.
