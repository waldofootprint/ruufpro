**Q: I'm not a tech guy. Can I actually do this?**
If you can type your business name and phone number, you can do this. We generate everything else — the design, the words, the SEO. Most roofers are live in under 2 minutes.

**Q: How is this different from Roofle?**
Roofle charges $350/mo plus a $2,000 setup fee — that's $6,200 in year one — for an estimate widget only. No website included. RuufPro gives you a free website plus an estimate widget for $99/mo with no setup fee.

**Q: What about the Google Online Estimates filter?**
Google now lets homeowners filter search results to only show roofers with online pricing. Our estimate widget qualifies your site for this filter, keeping you visible when competitors get hidden.

**Q: Can I use my own domain?**
Coming soon. Right now your site lives at yourname.ruufpro.com, which works great for getting started. Custom domain support is on our roadmap.

**Q: What if I already have a website?**
You can embed the estimate widget on your existing site — it works anywhere. Or try our free site alongside yours and see which generates more leads.

**Q: Do I own my leads?**
Yes. Every lead belongs to you. We never sell or share your leads with other roofers.

**Q: How does the estimate widget work?**
A homeowner enters their address. We use satellite imagery to measure their roof. They pick a material, and our calculator generates a ballpark estimate based on YOUR pricing. You set the prices — we do the math.
