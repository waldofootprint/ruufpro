## 1. CONTRACTOR+ (contractorplus.app)

### What They Offer
Contractor+ is an all-in-one field service management app (founded 2020) targeting small contractors across ALL trades — not roofing-specific.

**Pricing:**
| Plan | Price | Website Included? |
|------|-------|-------------------|
| Freedom (Free) | $0/mo | NO |
| Pro | $29/mo (1 user) | YES |
| Pro Team | $58/mo (2 users) + $20/mo per additional | YES |

**Website Features (Pro/Pro Team only):**
- Auto-generated local service area pages (SEO-optimized)
- Auto-generated service-specific pages per trade
- AI copywriter for business descriptions
- Custom domain with SSL
- Branded client portal (clients.yourwebsite.com)
- Blog functionality
- Claims "10 mins from zero to live" and "5x increased visibility"

### Marketing Strategy
- **Free tier as lead magnet:** The Freedom plan ($0) gets users into the ecosystem — CRM, estimates, invoices. Then upsell to Pro ($29) for the website.
- **App store distribution:** Strong presence on iOS/Android app stores
- **Review site SEO:** Well-represented on G2 (4.5+), Capterra, Software Advice, GetApp
- **Content marketing:** Blog posts on contractor business topics
- **NOT heavy on paid ads** — their growth seems organic/word-of-mouth + app store discovery

### User Sentiment
- Reviews consistently positive for ease of use and value for money
- Users praise the estimate templates and time savings
- No significant roofing-specific complaints found (because they're not roofing-specific)

### Threat Level to RuufPro: MODERATE
- They prove the "free tier + website upsell" model works
- But they're generic (all trades) — not roofing-optimized
- No satellite estimate widget
- Website quality is template-grade, not contractor-grade
- Their website is a FEATURE of their CRM, not the core product

### What We Can Learn
Their pricing ladder ($0 -> $29 -> $58) is smart. Getting contractors into a free CRM then upselling the website is the same playbook RuufPro uses (free website -> paid widget). They validate our model.

## 2. GOHIGHLEVEL / HIGHLEVEL — The Reseller Army

### How It Works
GoHighLevel is NOT a direct competitor — it's the platform BEHIND hundreds of marketing agencies that sell to roofers. Understanding GHL means understanding the entire agency ecosystem targeting roofing contractors.

**GHL's Own Pricing:**
| Plan | Price | Key Feature |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Starter | $97/mo | Single account |
| Unlimited | $297/mo | Unlimited sub-accounts |
| SaaS Pro | $497/mo | White-label + resell as own SaaS |

**What Agencies Do:**
1. Buy GHL at $297-$497/mo
2. White-label it with their branding
3. Sell pre-built "snapshots" (templates) to roofers
4. Charge roofers $297-$2,500/mo for the same platform

**GHL's Roofing Playbook:**
- Pre-built roofing-specific snapshot with lead funnels, booking pages, automated follow-ups, review request sequences
- Mobile-optimized service booking pages
- "Instant quote request forms" (NOT satellite-powered estimates — just forms)
- Automated text replies and follow-up workflows

### How GHL is Marketed to Roofers
- **Through agencies, NOT directly.** Roofers rarely know they're on GHL.
- Agencies brand it as their own proprietary "roofing marketing system"
- Common pitch: "We'll build you a website, run your ads, automate your follow-ups" for $500-2,500/mo
- **Robb Bailey** (GHL's most prominent evangelist) has trained 20,000+ agencies on the "Agency/SaaS Hybrid" model — including many targeting home services/roofing
- Active **Skool community** (GoHighLevel w/ Robb Bailey) with roofing niche discussions

### Why Roofing is GHL's #1 Niche
- Home services are "the undisputed top niche for GHL-based SaaS right now"
- Simple to understand, easy to replicate across cities
- One roofing snapshot can be sold to roofers in 100+ cities
- Roofing retainers ($500-$2,500/mo) make it highly profitable per client

### Threat Level to RuufPro: HIGH (indirect)
- GHL agencies are NOT competing with us on product — they're competing for the same CUSTOMERS
- Every roofer locked into a $500-$2,500/mo GHL agency is a roofer who won't need our free website
- BUT: most GHL agency clients are mid-size+ roofers ($2M+ revenue). Small roofers can't afford $500/mo retainers.
- The roofers burned by these agencies are our BEST customers — they want something simple and affordable

### What We Can Learn
- The GHL ecosystem proves roofers WILL pay for marketing tools
- The agency model's weakness is the trust issue — 2/3 of roofers are unhappy with marketing providers
- Our anti-agency positioning ("No salesperson. No contract. No retainer.") directly counters GHL agencies

## 3. NEW & EMERGING PLAYERS (Late 2025 - 2026)

### AI Website Builders Entering the Space
Several AI-powered website builders are now targeting contractors:

**Mobirise AI** — Free AI website builder that generates roofing company sites with tailored content, images, layouts. No coding needed. Threat: low (generic quality).

**Butternut AI** — Creates SEO-friendly professional websites with no coding. Free domain included. Threat: low (not roofing-specific).

**Replit** — "AI Roofing Website Builder" landing page. Threat: low (developer-focused tool, not for non-technical roofers).

**Assessment:** AI builders are proliferating but none are roofing-SPECIFIC with industry knowledge (service areas, roofing terminology, trust signals). They produce generic sites. RuufPro's roofing expertise is the differentiator.

### Estimate Widget Landscape Update

| Player | Pricing | Key Differentiator |
|--------|---------|-------------------|
| **Roofle RoofQuote PRO** | $350/mo + $2K setup OR $5,500/yr | Most established widget. 15K+ users. Owens Corning partnership. |
| **Roofr Instant Estimator** | $149/mo add-on (on top of $249-349/mo base) | Part of full CRM ecosystem. |
| **Instant Roofer** | Free for homeowners (lead-gen model) | Pairs homeowners with contractors. Roofers don't control it. |
| **eRoofQuote** | Free for homeowners | Address-based aerial measurements. Lead-gen for contractors. |
| **Jobber Roof Measurement** | Free tool | Uses EagleView imagery. Good for screening leads, not binding. |
| **Owens Corning Budget Your Roof** | Free widget for OC contractors | Manufacturer-backed. Educates homeowners on budget expectations. |
| **GAF QuickMeasure** | Part of GAF ecosystem | Manufacturer measurement tool. |
| **RuufPro (us)** | $99/mo (planned) | Cheapest embeddable widget with satellite data. Free website included. |

**Key Insight:** The widget market is fragmenting. Roofle dominates the premium end ($350/mo+). Roofr bundles it into their CRM. Free tools exist but they're lead-gen for the PLATFORM, not for the roofer. Nobody offers an affordable ($99/mo) widget that the roofer controls on their own site — that's our gap.

### ProLine CRM
- Newer entrant positioning as "the CRM that sells more roofs"
- Free tier with done-for-you setup
- Focus on communication (calling, texting, SMS sequences)
- Targets residential/retail roofers with small-to-mid teams
- NOT a website builder or estimate widget — complementary to RuufPro

### Owens Corning + Roofle Partnership
- Owens Corning offers an exclusive version of RoofQuote PRO to their contractor network
- This is a manufacturer-backed distribution channel we can't match
- OC also offers free web widgets (Budget Your Roof, Design EyeQ visualizer) to contractors in their network
- **Implication:** OC-certified contractors have free/discounted access to Roofle. Non-OC roofers are the better target for RuufPro.

### What Agencies Typically Include
- Custom website design and hosting
- SEO (local, on-page, content)
- Google Ads / PPC management
- Google Local Service Ads (LSA)
- Social media management
- Review generation/management
- Monthly reporting
- Some include CRM setup (usually GHL)

### Average Roofing Marketing Budgets
- **Small contractors (<$5M revenue):** 7-8% of revenue on marketing ($35K-$400K/yr)
- **Larger contractors:** 10-12% of revenue
- **SBA recommendation:** 7-8% for businesses under $5M
- **Industry trend:** Moving from 1-3% to 5-10% as digital becomes essential

### What Roofers Say About Agencies
- **2/3 of roofers are unhappy with their SEO/marketing providers**
- "High-pressure one-call-close sales tactics"
- Locked into long-term contracts they regret
- Paid $3-5K for websites that don't generate leads
- "Low barrier to entry into the marketing industry" means many agencies are unqualified
- Facebook ads "gone from a little-known marketing hack to oversaturated" — higher costs, fewer leads
- Many roofers can't track ROI and feel they're "throwing money away"

### RuufPro's Position Relative to Agencies
**We're NOT competing with agencies — we're the ALTERNATIVE for roofers who can't afford them.**

A roofer paying $3-15K/mo to Hook Agency is not our customer. Our customer is the roofer who:
- Can't afford $3K/mo for marketing
- Got burned by an agency and won't try again
- Just started and needs SOMETHING before they can afford an agency
- Wants to own their own website (not rent from an agency)

**Positioning:** "What agencies charge $3,000/month for, we give you for free."

## 6. WHAT ROOFERS COMPLAIN ABOUT (Marketing/Website Specific)

### Top Complaints About Current Solutions

**1. "Marketing agencies burned me" (MOST COMMON)**
- 2/3+ of roofers unhappy with marketing providers
- Paid thousands for websites that don't generate leads
- Locked into contracts they regret
- "All that does is throw money at the problem"
- Agencies overpromise, underdeliver, then disappear

**2. "It's too expensive for what I get"**
- Roofr's per-report fees ($13-19/report) punish growth
- Roofle's $6,200 first-year cost is prohibitive for small roofers
- Agency retainers of $3,000-15,000/mo are out of reach
- Paying for features they never use

**3. "Facebook ads don't work anymore"**
- "Gone from a little-known marketing hack to oversaturated"
- Higher costs, fewer leads
- "Low-quality Facebook ad agencies" generate useless leads
- "Dozens of leads, no quotes, and hours of wasted time"

**4. "I can't track what's working"**
- Without ROI tracking, marketing feels like gambling
- Can't identify most profitable lead sources
- Agencies don't provide transparent reporting

**5. "My website doesn't do anything"**
- 75% of users judge credibility based on website design
- Many roofers have outdated or non-existent sites
- Contact forms get ignored; no one calls back
- "Most roofing websites have problems because unqualified or cheapest people are hired to build them"

**6. "I don't have time to learn software"**
- On roofs 10 hours/day
- Not tech-savvy (identity, not just preference)
- Want phone to ring, not a dashboard to manage
- ServiceTitan/AccuLynx learning curves are prohibitive

**7. "I just need something simple that works"**
- Market is over-featured for small roofers
- Don't need CRM, material ordering, fleet tracking
- Want: website + phone rings + look professional
- The gap RuufPro fills exactly

### Common Marketing Mistakes Roofers Make
- Hiring agencies too early (before they have budget to sustain)
- No tracking = wasting money on ineffective campaigns
- Neglecting Google Business Profile optimization
- Ignoring SEO and local search
- Not responding to reviews (67% of homeowners rate reviews as very/extremely important)

## 7. GOOGLE'S "ONLINE ESTIMATES" FILTER — March 2026 Update

### Current Status
- **Launched:** December 2025 (quiet rollout)
- **Adoption:** 1 in 5 (20%) homeowners use the filter when searching for roofing services
- **Trend:** Growing — Demand IQ hosted a webinar March 19, 2026 titled "Capitalizing on Google's Shift to Online Estimates" calling it a "major shift"
- **Industries affected:** Roofing, HVAC, plumbing, electrical, windows, siding, solar

### What's Changed Since December 2025
- Google added "Online estimate" buttons directly in search results (not just a filter)
- Demand IQ and EagleView partnering to power instant estimates using AI + aerial imagery
- Industry webinars/articles increased dramatically in Q1 2026 — awareness is spreading fast
- RoofersCoffeeShop, Roofle, Roofr, Demand IQ all positioning their tools as THE way to qualify

### How to Qualify
1. Enable "Online estimates" attribute in Google Business Profile
2. Have a REAL online estimate experience on your website (not just a contact form)
3. Google checks for: guided questions, real pricing logic, immediate estimate ranges, user engagement (time on page, completion rates)
4. "Not 'fill out this form and we'll call you.' Not vague generalized pricing ranges." — Demand IQ

### Impact Data
- Contractors WITHOUT online estimates: invisible to 20% of high-intent searchers (and growing)
- Contractors WITH online estimates: visibility boost, algorithmic preference, better lead quality
- Case study: Maven Roofing lands "2+ extra jobs/month without extra work" from instant estimates
- "Years of SEO work, review gathering, and thousands of marketing dollars can suddenly stop paying off" — Demand IQ

### Who's Positioned to Benefit
| Platform | Their Pitch |
|----------|-------------|
| **Roofle** | Wrote the definitive blog post. "$350/mo and you qualify." |
| **Roofr** | "Your online estimates matter more than ever." $149/mo add-on. |
| **Demand IQ** | Powers estimates for solar/HVAC/roofing. Enterprise focus. |
| **Owens Corning** | Free Budget Your Roof widget for OC contractors. |
| **RuufPro** | **NOT YET POSITIONED.** We need to be. $99/mo widget = cheapest path to qualifying. |

### RuufPro's Opportunity
This is our single strongest marketing message. The pitch:

> "Google now hides roofers without online estimates. 1 in 5 homeowners use this filter. Roofle charges $350/mo to fix this. We charge $99/mo — and the website is free."

No other competitor combines the website + widget at this price. Our gap remains wide open.
